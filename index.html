In 2013, I realized lots of people in developing countries were being being taken advantage of by their governments and rich locals concerning the price exploitation and censorship of domain names. This happens because people dont understand how the system is run and organized. This project was devoted to providing greater access to the seemingly complicated legal structure that is The Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers.

Who am i?

I am an architecture school graduate, turned designer and developer currently residing in beautiful Portland, Oregon. I have realized that architecture and web development are not as different as most might think . My background enables me to approach web development from new and different perspectives and at the core of them both is my passion for design and technology. I am a tinkerer, and a curious soul that thrives on being able to learn new skills. I enjoy building things both physical and digital.
